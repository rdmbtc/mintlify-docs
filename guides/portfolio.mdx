Your Puls portfolio is a live dashboard of everything you have at stake — every open trade, every resolved outcome, and your overall financial performance on the platform. Whether you're managing a handful of positions or actively trading across dozens of markets, the Portfolio section gives you a clear picture of where you stand and how your predictions are playing out.

<Info>
  Portfolio values fluctuate in real time as market prices change. The current value shown for any open position reflects what the market thinks that position is worth right now — not what you paid for it. Final profit or loss is only realized when a market resolves.
</Info>

## Open Positions

The **Open Positions** tab lists every market where you currently hold shares. For each position, you'll see:

- **Market question** — The prediction market you traded on, with a link to the full market page
- **Your side** — Whether you hold YES or NO shares
- **Shares held** — The number of shares you own in this market
- **Average cost** — The average price per share you paid, accounting for any follow-on trades in the same market
- **Current value** — The real-time market value of your shares based on the current probability
- **Unrealized P&L** — Your current gain or loss compared to what you paid, updated live as prices move
- **Closing date** — When trading ends and the market moves toward resolution

Use this tab to keep track of how your active predictions are performing and decide whether to hold, add to a position, or exit early if the option is available.

## Resolved Positions

The **Resolved Positions** tab is your permanent trade history. Every market that has closed and received a final resolution appears here, whether you won, lost, or received a refund due to cancellation.

For each resolved trade, you'll find:

- **Market question** — The market that resolved, with a link to the resolution details
- **Your side** — The position you held (YES or NO)
- **Shares held** — How many shares you owned at resolution
- **Resolution outcome** — Whether the market resolved YES, NO, or N/A (cancelled)
- **Payout received** — The amount credited to your balance at resolution
- **Net profit / loss** — Your final gain or loss on the trade: payout minus original cost

Resolved positions are listed in reverse chronological order. Use this history to review your track record, identify patterns in your trading, and learn from both wins and losses.

## Portfolio Summary

At the top of the Portfolio page, the **Summary** panel gives you a high-level view of your overall account performance:

- **Total invested** — The cumulative amount you've put into open positions currently
- **Current value** — The real-time estimated value of all your open positions combined
- **Overall return** — Your net gain or loss across all resolved trades, expressed in dollars and as a percentage
- **Win rate** — The percentage of your resolved positions that ended in your favor

The summary updates continuously. Bookmark this page if you want a quick read on your portfolio health without diving into individual positions.

## Exiting a Position

Puls supports early exit on select markets, allowing you to sell your shares before the market resolves. If early exit is available for a market, you'll see a **Sell** button next to that position in the Open Positions tab.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Your Positions">
    Go to **Portfolio > Open Positions** to see all markets where you currently hold shares.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the Market to Exit">
    Find the market you want to sell out of and click the **Sell** button next to it. If no Sell button is visible, that market does not support early exit and your position will be held to resolution.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Your Sell Quantity">
    Select how many shares to sell. You can sell your entire position or just a portion — the input field lets you specify any amount up to the number of shares you hold.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the Proceeds">
    The panel shows your estimated proceeds based on the current market price. This amount may be higher or lower than your original cost depending on how the probability has moved since you bought in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the Sale">
    Click **Confirm Sale**. The proceeds are credited to your Puls balance immediately, and the position is removed from your Open Positions list.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Keep in mind that selling early locks in your current gain or loss — if the market later resolves in your favor, you won't receive the full $1.00 per share payout for shares you've already sold. You can always check the market detail page to confirm whether early exit is enabled before entering a position.

<Note>
  When a market resolves, your payout is credited to your Puls balance automatically — no action required. See the [Payouts guide](/guides/payouts) to learn how resolution payouts are calculated and how to withdraw your winnings.
</Note>
