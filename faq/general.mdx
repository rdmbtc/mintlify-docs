Whether you're brand new to prediction markets or just getting started with Puls, you're in the right place. Below you'll find answers to the questions we hear most often — from what Puls actually is to how you can reach our support team when you need a hand.

<Accordion title="What is Puls?">
  Puls is a prediction markets platform built around the tagline *The market for what happens next.* You trade on the outcomes of real-world events — politics, sports, finance, pop culture, and more — by buying shares that reflect your view of the probability of an outcome. If your prediction is correct when the market resolves, you win; if not, you lose the amount you staked.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What are prediction markets?">
  Prediction markets are exchange-based platforms where participants buy and sell shares tied to the outcome of future events. Prices move between $0.00 and $1.00 and represent the collective probability the crowd assigns to each outcome. Because prices reflect the wisdom of many informed traders, prediction markets are often remarkably accurate forecasting tools.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Who can use Puls?">
  Puls is open to anyone who meets our eligibility requirements: you must be of legal age in your jurisdiction, agree to our Terms of Service, and reside in a supported region. You'll need to complete identity verification before you can deposit funds or place trades.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is Puls available in my country?">
  Puls is available in a number of countries, but access depends on local laws and regulations around prediction markets and financial trading. During sign-up, we'll automatically check whether your region is supported. If your country isn't listed yet, keep an eye on our announcements — we're continuously working to expand availability.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is Puls free to use?">
  Creating an account and browsing markets on Puls is completely free. Trading fees may apply when you buy or sell shares — you can find the current details on our Fees page. There are no subscription charges or hidden costs to simply being a member.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I create an account?">
  Head to the Puls homepage and click **Sign Up**. You'll enter your email address, create a password, and verify your identity by providing some basic personal information. Once your account is verified, you can deposit funds and start trading right away.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How is Puls different from sports betting?">
  Traditional sports betting involves wagering against a bookmaker who sets fixed odds and profits from the margin. On Puls, you trade against other users in an open market, and prices are determined by collective supply and demand rather than a house. This means you can sell your position before a market resolves, react to new information in real time, and often find more accurate pricing.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Where can I get support?">
  You can reach our support team at any time via the **Help** link in the navigation menu or by emailing support@pulsmarket.tech. We also have a community forum where experienced traders share tips and answer questions. Our team typically responds to support tickets within one business day.
</Accordion>
