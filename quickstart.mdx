Getting started on Puls is straightforward. In just a few steps you'll have an active account, a funded balance, and your first trade on the books. This guide walks you through the entire process from sign-up to your first confirmed position.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Head to [pulsmarket.tech](https://pulsmarket.tech) and click **Sign Up**. Enter your email address and choose a secure password.

    Once you've submitted the form, check your inbox for a verification email from Puls and click the confirmation link. Verifying your email activates your account and gives you full access to all markets and platform features.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fund Your Balance">
    Before you can trade, you need to add funds to your Puls balance.

    1. Log in and navigate to **Account → Funding** in the top navigation menu.
    2. Select your preferred funding method and enter the amount you'd like to deposit.
    3. Confirm the transaction. Your balance will update as soon as the deposit is processed.

    Your Puls balance is what you use to buy shares in any market. You can add more funds at any time from the same Funding page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find a Market">
    With a funded balance, you're ready to find something to trade on.

    - Browse the **Markets** feed on the homepage to see all currently open markets, sorted by activity and closing date.
    - Use the **search bar** to find markets by keyword — try a topic you follow closely, like a sport, a country, or a specific event.
    - Filter by **category** (Politics, Sports, Finance, Technology, and more) to narrow down to areas where you have the most conviction.

    Click any market to see its full details, including the current YES/NO prices, resolution criteria, trading history, and closing date.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Place Your Trade">
    Once you've found a market you want to trade on, placing your position takes just a few seconds.

    1. On the market detail page, click **YES** if you believe the event will happen, or **NO** if you believe it won't.
    2. Enter the amount you want to stake in the order panel. You'll see a live preview of your estimated payout and the number of shares you'll receive at the current price.
    3. Review the order summary and click **Confirm Trade**.

    Your position is now open. You can monitor it at any time from your **Portfolio** page, and your payout will be credited automatically if the market resolves in your favor.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Trade responsibly. Only stake amounts you're comfortable with, and remember that all prediction market trades carry risk — even high-probability outcomes don't always resolve as expected. Start with smaller positions while you build familiarity with the platform and market dynamics.
</Note>
