## Get Started in Three Steps

New to Puls? You can go from sign-up to your first trade in just a few minutes. Here's how it works at a glance.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an Account">
    Sign up at [pulsmarket.tech](https://pulsmarket.tech) with your email address. Verify your account to unlock full access to all markets and features.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fund Your Balance">
    Navigate to **Account → Funding** and add funds to your Puls balance. Your balance is used to buy shares in prediction markets.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Place Your First Trade">
    Browse the markets feed, pick an event you have a view on, and buy YES or NO shares. If the event resolves in your favor, you earn a payout.
  </Step>
</Steps>
