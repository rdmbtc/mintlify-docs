Your account settings give you full control over how your profile looks, how Puls communicates with you, and how your information is shared with other traders. You can access everything described on this page by navigating to **Account > Settings** after signing in.

## Profile Settings

Your public profile is what other traders see when they view your activity on the platform. Keep it up to date so you're recognizable within the Puls community.

- **Display name** — the name shown on your trades, comments, and leaderboard entries. You can change this at any time from **Account > Settings > Profile**.
- **Avatar** — upload a profile picture to personalize your account. Supported formats are JPG, PNG, and GIF, with a maximum file size of 5 MB.
- **Bio** — add a short bio (up to 160 characters) to tell other traders a bit about yourself. This appears on your public profile page.

Click **Save Changes** after updating any of these fields to apply your edits.

## Notification Preferences

Puls can alert you about activity across the platform so you never miss a market resolution or a meaningful price move. You can enable or disable each notification type independently for both **email** and **in-app** channels.

To manage your preferences, go to **Account > Settings > Notifications**.

<Accordion title="Market Resolution Alerts">
  Receive a notification whenever a market you've traded in is resolved. These alerts confirm the outcome and tell you whether your position was a win or a loss, along with your updated balance. We recommend keeping this one enabled so you always know when your winnings are available.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Price Movement Alerts">
  Get notified when the price on a market you're following moves significantly — useful for spotting momentum shifts or re-entry opportunities. You can set the sensitivity of these alerts (for example, only notify on moves greater than 10%) from the notification settings panel.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="New Market Alerts">
  Be the first to know when Puls adds new markets in categories you care about. You can choose which categories trigger new-market notifications so you only hear about topics that are relevant to you.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Account Activity Alerts">
  Stay informed about important account events, including successful deposits, withdrawal confirmations, password changes, and new sign-ins from unrecognized devices. We strongly recommend keeping account activity alerts enabled at all times for security purposes.
</Accordion>

<Tip>
  Enable notifications for every market you're actively trading on. Real-time resolution alerts ensure you know the moment your position pays out — so you can reinvest your winnings without delay.
</Tip>

## Security Settings

You can update your password and manage two-factor authentication (2FA) from **Account > Settings > Security**.

**Change your password** — enter your current password, then set a new one. Choose something unique to Puls and at least 12 characters long. Your session will remain active after a password change, but other devices will be signed out.

**Two-factor authentication (2FA)** — 2FA adds a one-time code requirement on top of your password at each sign-in. To enable it, click **Set Up 2FA** and follow the prompts to link an authenticator app such as Google Authenticator or Authy. Once enabled, you can also generate backup codes to store safely in case you lose access to your authenticator device.

## Privacy

Control what other traders can see when they look at your Puls profile.

- **Profile visibility** — set your profile to **Public** (visible to all users) or **Private** (only visible to you). A private profile hides your display name and bio from other traders.
- **Trade history visibility** — choose whether your past trades are visible on your public profile. Hiding your trade history does not affect your own access to it — you can always see your full history under **Account > Funding > Transaction History**.

Changes to your privacy settings take effect immediately and apply to all future profile views.

## Closing Your Account

If you'd like to permanently delete your Puls account, contact our support team at **support@pulsmarket.tech** with the subject line "Account Deletion Request." Include the email address associated with your account.

<Warning>
  Before your account can be closed, any **open positions must be resolved or manually exited**. Our support team will walk you through this process and confirm how your remaining available balance will be returned to you. Account deletion is permanent — your trade history, profile, and balance cannot be recovered once the process is complete.
</Warning>
