Getting started on Puls takes just a few minutes. Once your account is set up, you'll have full access to prediction markets where you can trade on the outcomes of real-world events — from politics and sports to science and culture.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up">
    Head to [pulsmarket.tech](https://pulsmarket.tech) and click **Sign Up** in the top-right corner. Enter your email address and choose a strong password, then click **Create Account** to continue.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Your Email">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from Puls. Click the confirmation link inside to verify your address. If you don't see it within a few minutes, check your spam or junk folder — or request a new verification email from the sign-in page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Your Profile">
    After verifying your email, you'll be prompted to complete your profile. Add a **display name** that other traders will see, and optionally upload a **profile picture** to personalize your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the Rules">
    Before you start trading, read and accept the Puls platform terms of service and trading guidelines. These outline how markets work, what conduct is expected, and how disputes are handled.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fund Your Account">
    With your account ready, add funds to your trading balance so you can start placing trades. See the [Funding guide](/account/funding) for step-by-step instructions on depositing and managing your balance.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Account Security

Keeping your account secure protects both your identity and your trading balance. Follow these best practices from the moment you sign up.

**Choose a strong password.** Use a password that is at least 12 characters long and includes a mix of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols. Avoid reusing passwords from other sites.

**Enable two-factor authentication (2FA).** Once your account is active, navigate to **Account > Settings > Security** and turn on 2FA. With 2FA enabled, you'll be asked to enter a one-time code from your authenticator app each time you sign in, adding a second layer of protection even if your password is ever compromised.

**Never share your credentials.** Puls will never ask for your password via email or chat. If you receive a message requesting your login details, treat it as a phishing attempt and do not respond.

<Note>
  You must verify your email address before you can place any trades on Puls. If your email is unverified, trading features will remain locked until you complete the verification step.
</Note>
