Your Puls balance is what you use to enter trades across all prediction markets on the platform. You can add funds at any time, track your available balance in real time, and withdraw your winnings directly back to your payment method whenever you're ready.

## Adding Funds

Depositing into your Puls account is straightforward and takes effect immediately.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Funding page">
    Sign in to your account and navigate to **Account > Funding**. Click **Deposit** to open the deposit flow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose an amount">
    Enter the amount you'd like to deposit. Minimum deposit amounts may apply. There is no maximum single-deposit limit, though large deposits may be subject to additional verification.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a payment method">
    Choose your preferred payment method. Puls accepts credit and debit cards, and additional payment methods may be available depending on your region. You'll see all supported options presented during the deposit flow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the deposit">
    Review the deposit details and click **Confirm**. Funds are added to your available balance instantly once the transaction is approved.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Your Balance

Understanding how your balance works helps you manage your trading activity effectively.

Your **available balance** is the portion of your funds that is free to use — you can place new trades or withdraw this amount at any time.

When you enter a trade, the cost of that position is moved into your **locked balance**. Locked funds are held against your open positions and are not available to withdraw until the market resolves or you exit the position. Once a market resolves in your favor, your winnings — including your returned stake — are released back into your available balance automatically.

You can view both your available balance and your locked balance at any time from the **Account > Funding** page.

## Withdrawing Funds

You can withdraw your available balance back to a verified payment method at any time.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Withdraw page">
    Navigate to **Account > Funding** and click **Withdraw**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the withdrawal amount">
    Type in the amount you'd like to withdraw. Minimum withdrawal amounts may apply, and you can only withdraw funds from your available balance — locked funds are not eligible until the associated positions are resolved or closed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your payment method">
    Choose the payment method you'd like to receive the funds on. The available withdrawal options are shown during the withdrawal flow and may vary depending on your region and the payment method you used to deposit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the withdrawal">
    Review the details and click **Confirm Withdrawal**. You'll receive an email confirmation once the request is submitted.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Withdrawals are typically processed within **1–3 business days**. The exact timing depends on your payment provider, and it may take additional time for funds to appear in your account. You'll receive an email notification when your withdrawal has been processed.
</Info>

## Transaction History

Every deposit and withdrawal is logged in your transaction history so you can keep track of your account activity.

To view your history, go to **Account > Funding** and scroll to the **Transaction History** section. Each entry shows the transaction type (deposit or withdrawal), the amount, the date, and the current status. You can use this log to reconcile your records or follow up on any pending transactions.

<Warning>
  Only deposit funds you can genuinely afford to lose. Prediction markets involve risk — outcomes are uncertain and you may not get back what you put in. Trade responsibly, set a budget that works for you, and never deposit money that you rely on for essential expenses.
</Warning>
