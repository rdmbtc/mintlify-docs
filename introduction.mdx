Puls is a prediction markets platform built for anyone who wants to trade on real-world outcomes — from election results and sports championships to economic indicators and breaking news. If you have a view on what happens next, Puls gives you a market to express it, a price that reflects the crowd's collective wisdom, and a payout when you're right.

## What Are Prediction Markets?

Prediction markets are financial markets where the prices of contracts represent the probability of a future event occurring. Instead of trading stocks or currencies, you trade on outcomes: will a specific event happen or not? Prices move between 0 and 100, where the current price reflects what the market collectively believes the chances of that outcome are. When the event resolves, winning shares pay out at full value and losing shares pay out nothing.

## Why Puls?

Puls is built around reliability, speed, and a clean trading experience that works for both first-time traders and experienced market participants. Here's what sets it apart:

- **Real-time markets with fair pricing.** Puls uses an automated market maker that ensures you can always enter or exit a position at a fair price, even in low-liquidity markets.
- **Clear resolution criteria.** Every market on Puls is backed by transparent, pre-defined resolution rules tied to official sources — so you always know exactly what you're trading on.
- **Built for all experience levels.** Whether you're placing your first trade or managing a large portfolio, Puls scales with you. The interface is simple to navigate but powerful enough for serious traders.
